Agreement Engine

The Agreement Engine is a software model for the creation, representation, and enforcement of all different kinds of agreements. We are currently prototyping a model of enforcement that uses a tokenized form of social capital we call TSC. To read more about what Agreement Engine is and how TSC works, check out our blog post.

To follow Agreement Engine's development, take a look at our progress and development goals. You can find our code base and contribute on GitHub.

Agreement Engine is part of the larger Metagovernance Project. Check out metagov.org for more information!
